# Workshop-Diy Islamic Apps 🕌🌙

A **multilingual hub** for Workshop-Diy Islamic & Arabic apps — interactive books, prayer tools, Arabic language learning, and more.

> 🇫🇷 *Un hub multilingue pour toutes les apps islamiques et arabes de Workshop-Diy.*
>
> 🇸🇦 *مركز متعدد اللغات لجميع التطبيقات الإسلامية والعربية من Workshop-Diy.*

---

## Features

- 🎨 **12 Themes** — including Islamic, Alhambra, Iznik, Zellige, Arabesque
- 🌍 **3 Languages** — English, Francais, العربية (with full RTL support)
- 🔍 **Smart Search** — Fuzzy search across all apps
- 📂 **4 Categories** — Arabic & Islamic, Books & Learning, AI, Tools
- ⭐ **Favorites** — Star your favorite apps (saved locally)
- 🎉 **Fun Interactions** — Confetti, sound effects, jokes
- 📱 **Responsive** — Mobile-first, touch-friendly

---

## App Categories

| Category | Description |
